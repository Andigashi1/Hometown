Top three activities to do in Prizren

Visit historical and cultural monuments

The remaining monuments in Prizren are a lot by number and lots of them are built in different eras by different empires/tribes

Attend the annual DOKUFEST festival

Dokufest is an international documentary and short film festival held anually during August. Its best to visit the city during this time.

Go skiing in Brezovica ski resort

Brezovica is a ski resort located in the mountains of Prizren and is one of the most visited winter tourist destination
